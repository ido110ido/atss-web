How It Works

Three steps.
Zero hassle.

Set it up once and forget about it. The bot handles everything else while you focus on running your business.

01

Connect

We integrate with your existing logistics system, WMS, or carrier feeds. No rip-and-replace — a lightweight connection alongside what you already have.

02

Monitor

The bot watches every active shipment 24/7. It polls carrier events and store check-in data, looking for the moment any order lands at a location.

03

Notify

The instant an arrival is detected, a WhatsApp message is sent to whoever needs to know — store manager, logistics team, or the owner directly.
